SEPHORA
SQUAD perks

As a Sephora Squad member...

You’ll enter a yearlong, paid partnership with Sephora where you’ll get to work with Sephora’s brands, star on our social channels, attend exclusive events, and meet industry experts and new beauty besties.

Check out the full range of benefits below.
All. The. Beauty.
You’ll receive gratis boxes packed with buzzed-about products and limited-edition Sephora Squad gear throughout the year, plus you’ll get early access to some of the industry’s most highly anticipated launches.
Get featured & grow your influence
We look to our Sephora Squad for original content to feature, whether it’s a TikTok video shot in your living room, a campaign shoot in our LA studio, or a cameo on a Times Square billboard.
Meet industry leaders
Grow your network by connecting with Squad Mentors, Sephora team members, and even celebrity brand founders. From incredible in-person gatherings to digital brand events and everything in between, there’s always an opportunity to mingle.
Professional development
Get experience on set and take advantage of professional equipment at our LA studio Creator Lounge. Plus, receive one-on-one advice and training from industry experts at monthly Office Hours with Sephora and Fohr team members.
Give back
Get involved with Sephora's equity partners, Charity Rewards program, and diversity and inclusion efforts. We are always looking for new ways to give back to the community.
Alumni perks
Once you’re in, the perks keep coming. Even after your yearlong partnership is up, you’ll still have access to trainings, gratis, brand events, local meet-ups, potential campaign opportunities, and more.

FREQUENTLY ASKED questions
How long will I be a part of the Sephora Squad?
The Sephora Squad partnership will last 12 months. New Sephora Squad creators will be selected each year.
Does it matter how many followers I have?
No. We value unique, unfiltered, sorry-not-sorry storytellers, no matter the number of followers they have.
What type of creator is Sephora Squad looking for?
We are looking for passionate creators who have an active Instagram or TikTok account and an engaged community of followers. A passion for beauty and Sephora is a plus! We welcome influencers of all races, ethnicities, religions, gender identities, body types, sexual orientations, and abilities to apply to be a part of the Sephora Squad.
I’m a Sephora employee, can I apply?
Yes! We encourage Sephora team members to apply if you’ve worked for Sephora for at least three months, are in good standing with your manager or supervisor, and have an active Instagram or TikTok account with an engaged community of followers. Sephora at Kohl’s employees who work at Sephora counters are not considered Sephora employees and should not identify themselves as such in their application. Sephora corporate employees are not eligible to apply.
How old do I have to be to apply?
You must be 18 or older.
Can influencers around the world apply?
How can I prepare to fill out my application?
You’ll need a high-resolution headshot photo (no larger than 10MB, a JPEG file type, and a square format), links to your active social media accounts, and your authentic voice!
Can I view my testimonials?
Applicants cannot directly view the testimonials they receive. Testimonials are only available to those who submit them and a panel of judges.
